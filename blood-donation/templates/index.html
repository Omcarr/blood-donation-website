Step 3: Prioritize a healthy diet

Before donating blood, have iron-rich foods like red meat, fish, poultry, beans, spinach, iron-fortified cereals, or raisins to ensure a healthy donation.

Step 4: Be Well Rested and Hydrate Regularly

Prior to your appointment, make sure you are well-rested and properly hydrated for a smooth donation process.
